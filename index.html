The SCP Foundation is a collaborative fictional universe centered around a secretive organization that contains anomalous entities and phenomena.
The SCP Foundation is a clandestine international organization tasked with securing, containing, and protecting anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena that violate natural law

The SCP Foundation is depicted as a global entity that operates outside the control of any government.
